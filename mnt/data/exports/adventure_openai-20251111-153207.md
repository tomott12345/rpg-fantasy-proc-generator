# Missing Person in Riftmarch: The Villain

## Summary
In the ash desert of Riftmarch, a paladin turned undead guardian is suspected as the missing person. As adventurers delve into the Howling Crypt, a rival faction—the Scarlet Covenant—seeks to control the guardian for their own dark purposes. The party must navigate this treacherous landscape, contend with necromantic zealots, and decide the fate of the cursed soul.

### Quest Overview
- **Type:** Missing Person
- **Theme:** Ruin Arcane
- **Complication:** A rival faction interferes.
- **Reward:** Treasure Tier 1d6

## Adventure Hooks
1. **Hook A:** Survivors report hearing their names spoken by a spectral guardian.
2. **Hook B:** The Scarlet Covenant offers a bounty for the capture of the guardian.
3. **Hook C:** Local tomb-robbers disappear; one returns bearing a terrible curse.

## Locations

### High Vale City
- **Type:** Hamlet
- **Population:** 90
- **Defense:** 1
- **Market Size:** Tiny
- **Description:** Built atop ancient ruins, this fog-laden hamlet is a hub for relic traders. Mysterious occurrences and whispers of the dead are common.

### The Howling Crypt
- **Depth:** 3
- **CR Guess:** 2
- **Description:** A cursed cliffside tomb where green fire flickers from ancient runes. It is said to hold the soul of a once-noble paladin.

## Monster Encounters

### The Bound Guardian
- **Type:** Undead (Sapient)
- **CR:** 3
- **Description:** A skeletal warden bound by spectral chains, it was once a paladin.
- **Abilities:**
  - **Spectral Chains (Recharge 5–6):** 10-ft AoE restrain, DC 13 STR save.
  - **Voice of the Fallen:** Speaks Common; remembers oaths.
- **Behavior:** Will parley if approached respectfully; desires release.

### Scarlet Covenant Agents
- **Type:** Humanoid Cultists
- **CR:** 2
- **Description:** Necromantic zealots seeking to enslave the guardian.
- **Abilities:**
  - **Necrotic Bolt:** 1d8+2 necrotic, 60 ft.
  - **Fanatical Zeal**

## Resolution Paths

1. **Slay the Guardian:** The crypt's relics are yours, but the Scarlet Covenant's influence grows.
2. **Free the Soul:** Invoke the true name at the altar, releasing the guardian and appeasing the spirits.
3. **Bargain with the Covenant:** Forge an uneasy alliance, gaining their resources but at what cost?

### Outcome
Riftmarch’s fate hinges on the party’s choice, determining whether the region will be liberated from its spectral curse or fall under the sway of necromantic zeal.
